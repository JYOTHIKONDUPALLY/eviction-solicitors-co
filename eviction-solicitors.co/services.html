Our Legal Services

Eviction Notices

We provide professional drafting and service of legally compliant eviction notices, including Section 8 and Section 21. Our experts ensure your documentation meets all statutory requirements, avoiding costly delays or legal challenges.

For official government guidance, visit the UK eviction process page .

Notice Drafting & Service

Precision-drafted notices delivered correctly the first time. We ensure each notice is tailored to your situation and served in accordance with legal standards to protect your rights as a landlord.

Court Representation

Our eviction solicitors provide full legal representation at possession hearings across England and Wales. We ensure all court paperwork is in order, represent your interests effectively, and guide you through every step of the eviction process. With experienced legal advocacy, landlords significantly improve their success rate in court proceedings.

Bailiff Enforcement

When tenants fail to vacate even after a possession order, we arrange bailiff enforcement to legally recover your property. From requesting a county court bailiff to escalating to High Court enforcement officers (HCEOs), we handle the process quickly and legally—minimizing delays, stress, and financial losses for landlords.

Section 21 Notice

Our specialist eviction solicitors provide legally compliant Section 21 notices, helping landlords regain possession of their property without giving a reason. We ensure every notice is properly served, aligned with the latest UK legislation, including deposit protection rules and prescribed information requirements—minimizing the risk of dismissal in court and saving time and legal fees.

Legal Advice & Actions

Whether you're dealing with a non-compliant tenant or facing complex property disputes, we offer practical legal advice and tailored strategies to help you resolve issues quickly and effectively.

Section 8 Notice

If your tenant is breaching the terms of their tenancy—whether due to rent arrears, property damage, or antisocial behaviour—our team helps you serve a strong and accurate Section 8 notice. We prepare the appropriate legal grounds under the Housing Act 1988 and support you with evidence and documentation to build a robust case for possession.

Rent Arrears Recovery

We assist landlords in recovering unpaid rent through legal claims and enforcement action. Our team ensures proper documentation and representation to maximise recovery success.

Tenancy Agreement Reviews

Our legal experts help you draft or review tenancy agreements that are watertight, fair, and fully compliant with the latest rental laws. We ensure your contracts protect your rights as a landlord and reduce the risk of future disputes—covering clauses like rent reviews, notice periods, deposit handling, and more.

Legal Compliance Guidance

Stay on top of your legal obligations as a landlord with our compliance services. We advise on deposit protection schemes, property licensing, energy efficiency standards, and health and safety requirements. Avoid costly fines, tenant disputes, and legal complications by partnering with experts who know the ever-changing regulations inside out.
