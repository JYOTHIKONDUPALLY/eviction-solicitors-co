Housing Disrepair Counterclaims in Eviction Proceedings (England & Wales – 2025 Guide)

When landlords begin possession proceedings, tenants often raise a counterclaim for housing disrepair. This legal defence can reduce rent arrears, delay eviction, or even dismiss proceedings. Landlords and tenants alike must understand rights, obligations, and court procedures under the Housing Act 1988 and Landlord and Tenant Act 1985. Our expert service helps you prepare, defend, and respond effectively to disrepair claims.
